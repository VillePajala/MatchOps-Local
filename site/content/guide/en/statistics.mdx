The Statistics Modal is your central hub for viewing and analyzing performance data across games, seasons, tournaments, and individual players.

## Opening Statistics

Access statistics by clicking the **Stats** button in the control bar or menu.

## Tabs Overview

The modal has five tabs, each showing data at a different aggregation level:

- **Current Game** - Stats for the active game only
- **Season** - Aggregated stats across season games
- **Tournament** - Aggregated stats across tournament games
- **Overall** - Combined stats across ALL games
- **Player** - Detailed individual player statistics and career data

<Callout type="info">
Filters reset when you switch between tabs. Each tab starts with clean filter state.
</Callout>

---

## Current Game Tab

Shows statistics for the game you're currently viewing.

### What You See

- **Game info** - Teams, score, location, time, periods
- **Player stats table** - Goals and assists for players selected in this game only
- **Goal events** - Editable list of goals with time, scorer, and assister
- **Personnel summary** - Coaches and staff assigned to the game
- **Game notes** - Your recorded observations (editable)

### Editing Goals

Click any goal event to edit it:
- Change the scorer or assister
- Adjust the time
- Delete the goal entirely

Changes update the score automatically.

### Filters

No filters on this tab - it shows only the current game's data.

---

## Season Tab

Shows aggregated statistics across all games in a season (excluding tournament games).

### What You See

**When a specific season is selected:**
- Team Performance Card showing that season's record
- Player stats table with season totals

**When "All Seasons" is selected:**
- Aggregate stats card showing totals across all seasons
- Combined player stats

### Filters

**Always visible:**
- **Season selector** - Choose a specific season or "All Seasons"

**In collapsible panel (tap "Filters"):**
- **Team** - Filter by specific team, "All Teams", or "Legacy Games" (pre-team games)
- **Game Type** - Soccer, Futsal, or All
- **Gender** - Boys, Girls, or All
- **Club Season** - Filter by period (e.g., "24/25")

---

## Tournament Tab

Shows aggregated statistics across tournament games (excluding season games).

### What You See

Same as Season tab but for tournament context:
- Team Performance Card for selected tournament
- Player stats aggregated across tournament games

### Filters

**Always visible:**
- **Tournament selector** - Choose a specific tournament or "All Tournaments"

**In collapsible panel:**
- **Series** - Only appears if the selected tournament has series/groups defined. Filter to a specific series within the tournament.
- **Team** - Filter by team
- **Game Type** - Soccer, Futsal, or All
- **Gender** - Boys, Girls, or All
- **Club Season** - Filter by period

<Callout type="tip">
The Series filter only appears when you select a specific tournament that has series configured. It won't show for "All Tournaments" or tournaments without series.
</Callout>

---

## Overall Tab

Shows combined statistics across ALL games - both seasons and tournaments.

### What You See

- Team Performance Card with all-time stats
- Optional team assessment averages (if you've rated games)
- Player stats table for all players across all games

### Filters

**Always visible:**
- **Team selector** - Always shown on this tab with three options:
  - **All Teams** - Stats across all your teams combined
  - **Legacy Games** - Games created before team system (no team assigned)
  - **Specific team** - Stats for one team only

**In collapsible panel:**
- **Game Type** - Soccer, Futsal, or All
- **Gender** - Boys, Girls, or All
- **Club Season** - Filter by period

---

## Player Tab

The most feature-rich tab - shows comprehensive individual player statistics and career data.

### Player Selection

Use the searchable dropdown to select a player. You can search by name or nickname. The list includes all players from your roster.

### Summary Stats

Shows four key metrics in a card:
- **Games Played** - Total games
- **Goals** - Total goals + per-game average
- **Assists** - Total assists + per-game average
- **Points** - Goals + Assists combined + per-game average

### Filter Status Indicator

When Club Season filter is active, a blue banner shows: "Viewing stats for selected club season only (X of Y games)" - helping you understand you're seeing filtered data.

### Filters

**In collapsible panel:**
- **Game Type** - Soccer, Futsal, or All
- **Gender** - Boys, Girls, or All
- **Club Season** - Filter by period

<Callout type="info">
Team filter is not shown on Player tab - player stats are inherently player-specific across all their teams.
</Callout>

### External Games Section

Record games the player played for other teams or in other leagues:

**Add external game with:**
- Season or Tournament (optional - for organizing)
- Home/Away/Neutral indicator
- External team name and opponent
- Score
- Game date
- Stats: Games, Goals, Assists
- Fair Play Card checkbox
- "Include in Season/Tournament stats" toggle
- Optional note

**Include in Season/Tournament toggle:**
- When ON: External game stats count toward season/tournament aggregates
- When OFF: Only counts toward player's overall career stats

Existing adjustments show in a list with edit/delete options.

### Game Log & Trends

**Metric selector** - Choose what to visualize:
- Goals & Assists (dual-line sparkline chart)
- Goals only (area chart)
- Assists only (area chart)
- Points (area chart)
- Assessment metrics (area charts)

Charts show the player's performance trends over time.

### Performance Ratings Section

If you've recorded player assessments:

- **Individual metric ratings** - Intensity, Courage, Duels, Technique, Creativity, Decisions, Awareness, Teamwork, Fair Play, Impact
- **Overall rating** - Combined rating
- **Average rating** - Weighted average across metrics
- **Rated games count** - How many games have assessments

**Demand Correction toggle:**
- When enabled, ratings from harder opponents count more
- Weights the averages by game difficulty

**Assessment trends** - Mini charts showing how each metric changed over time

**Assessment notes** - List of notes you've added during games

### Season/Tournament Performance

Breakdown tables showing:
- Per-season stats (GP, goals, assists, points, fair play cards)
- Per-tournament stats (same metrics + tournament winner indicator)

### Game-by-Game Breakdown

Individual game rows showing:
- **Result strip** - Color-coded: green (win), red (loss), gray (draw)
- **Teams and opponent**
- **Date**
- **Goals and assists** - Color-coded numbers
- **Badges:**
  - EXT (purple) - External game
  - FP (green) - Fair Play Card received
  - Futsal (orange) - Futsal game

Click any game row to navigate to that game's full statistics (except external games).

## Team Performance Card

Appears on Season, Tournament, and Overall tabs.

### Metrics Shown

- **Games Played** - Total count
- **Record** - Win-Loss-Tie format (e.g., "12-3-2")
- **Win %** - Percentage of games won
- **Goal Difference** - Goals for minus goals against (green if positive, red if negative)
- **Goals For** - Total scored
- **Goals Against** - Total conceded
- **Avg GF** - Average goals scored per game
- **Avg GA** - Average goals conceded per game
- **Last Game Date** - When shown

### Specific vs All

**When viewing a specific season/tournament:**
- Shows one Team Performance Card for that context only

**When viewing "All Seasons" or "All Tournaments":**
- Shows aggregate stats card with combined totals across all contexts

---

## Player Stats Table

Appears on Current Game, Season, Tournament, and Overall tabs.

### Columns

- **Player** - Name (clickable to view details)
- **GP** - Games Played
- **Goals** - Total goals scored
- **Assists** - Total assists
- **Pts** - Points (Goals + Assists)
- **Avg** - Average points per game

### Sorting

Click any column header to sort. Click again to reverse. Sorting indicator arrows show current sort state.

### Row Click

Click any player row to:
1. Switch to Player tab
2. Select that player
3. View their detailed career stats

### Totals Row

Bottom row shows aggregate totals across all displayed players.

---

## Club Season Filter

Filters games by club/academic season period (e.g., October to May).

### Setup Required

Club Season filter requires configuration in Settings:
- Set season start date (month-day)
- Set season end date (month-day)

If not configured, the filter shows as disabled with a settings button to configure it.

### Options

- **All Periods** - Shows all games regardless of season
- **Specific periods** - e.g., "23/24", "24/25"
- **Off-season** - Games outside the configured season period

---

## Exporting Statistics

Export buttons appear in the modal footer. Available exports depend on the active tab:

- **Current Game** - Single game data (events, player stats, notes)
- **Season** - Season aggregate stats
- **Tournament** - Tournament aggregate stats
- **Overall** - Full aggregate stats across all games
- **Player** - Individual player career data

Exports are saved as Excel (.xlsx) files.

## Common Use Cases

### Post-Game Review

1. Stats opens to Current Game tab automatically
2. Verify goal events are correct
3. Check player contributions
4. Add game notes if needed

### Season Progress Check

1. Switch to Season tab
2. Select your season from dropdown
3. Review team record and win percentage
4. Check top scorers in player table
5. Click player rows for individual details

### Player Development Tracking

1. Switch to Player tab
2. Select player from dropdown
3. Review career stats and trends
4. Check assessment ratings over time
5. Use Club Season filter to compare periods
6. Add external games if they played elsewhere

### Tournament Analysis

1. Switch to Tournament tab
2. Select tournament
3. Use Series filter to compare group performance
4. Review team stats and player contributions

<Callout type="tip">
Click any player row in the stats table to quickly jump to their detailed Player tab view with full career data.
</Callout>
