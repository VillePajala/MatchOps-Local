Learn how to use MatchOps Local effectively during live matches to track events and manage your team.

## The Game Interface

When a game is active, you'll see:

- **Field View** - Your lineup positioned on the pitch
- **Timer** - Current game time and period
- **Score** - Live score display
- **Player Bar** - Players at the top, ready for selection

## Recording Events

### Goals

When a goal is scored:

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Timer Overlay">
Tap the timer display in the control bar to open the Timer Overlay.
</Step>

<Step title="Log the Goal">
Use the **Log Goal** button for your team's goals, or **Opponent +1** for the opposition.
</Step>

<Step title="Record Details">
For your team's goals, select:
- Goalscorer (required - select from your roster)
- Assist player (optional)
- Game time (auto-filled from timer)
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm">
Click **Log Goal** - the score updates and the event is logged.
</Step>
</Steps>

You can also **edit or delete** recorded goals later through the Goal Log modal or Game Settings.

### Substitutions

Bench players are shown aligned at the side of the field. To make a substitution:

1. Tap a bench player at the side to select them
2. Tap on the field position where you want them
3. The previous player moves to the sideline automatically
4. Time played is tracked for both players

<Callout type="info">
Substitution timing is recorded automatically based on the game clock.
</Callout>

### Fair Play Cards

To award a Fair Play Card:

1. Open the menu (☰) and select **Game Settings**
2. Scroll to the Fair Play Card section
3. Select the player being recognized from the dropdown
4. The card is saved automatically

<Callout type="tip">
Fair Play Cards are **positive** awards for good sportsmanship - not disciplinary cards. Only one player per game can receive the Fair Play Card.
</Callout>

### Game Notes

You can add notes to capture important moments during the game using the notes feature.

## Timer Management

### Basic Controls

- **Play/Pause** - Toggle the timer using the play button (▶/⏸)
- **Period End** - Click to end the current period and advance to the next
- **Large Timer** - Tap the timer to expand to full-screen view for better visibility
- **Quick Goals** - Use the goal buttons in the timer overlay for fast scoring

### Substitution Interval Tracker

The timer includes a smart substitution reminder system:

1. **Set Interval** - Configure how often you want to rotate players (e.g., every 5 minutes)
2. **Visual Alerts** - The display changes color as substitution time approaches:
   - Normal (white) - Plenty of time
   - Warning (orange) - Substitution time approaching
   - Due (red) - Time to make substitutions
3. **Confirm Button** - Click "Confirm Substitution Made" when you complete a rotation
4. **Reset** - The timer resets and begins tracking the next interval

<Callout type="tip">
The substitution tracker helps ensure fair playing time for all players, especially in youth leagues.
</Callout>

### Period Transitions

At halftime or period end:

1. Pause the timer
2. Review period statistics
3. Make lineup changes
4. Click to advance to the next period
5. Press play to start the new period

### Stoppage Time

For stoppage time at the end of a period:

1. Keep the timer running past the period length
2. End the period manually when stoppage time is complete
3. The actual time played is recorded

## Making Adjustments

### Formation Changes

During the game, you can rearrange players on the field:

**Option 1: Drag**
- Drag a player disc to a new position on the field

**Option 2: Tap to swap**
- Tap a player to select them, then tap another player disc to swap positions

Changes happen instantly and you can use **Undo/Redo** buttons to reverse them.

### Field Tools Panel

Access additional tools from the control bar:

- **Place All Players** (⊞) - Auto-position players using formation presets (3v3 to 11v11)
- **Add Opponent** - Place opponent player markers on the field
- **Clear Drawings** - Remove all drawings from the field
- **Reset Field** - Return all players to bench

<Callout type="info">
**Quick Save** is available from the menu (☰) under Game Management.

</Callout>

### Tactics Mode

For more detailed tactical adjustments, use the **Tactics Mode**:

1. Click the **Tactics Mode** button
2. Draw plays, formations, or instructions
3. Place tactical discs (home/opponent/goalie)
4. Position the ball on the field
5. Show players during halftime discussions
6. See [Tactics Board](/guide/tactics-board) for detailed instructions

<Callout type="info">
The Tactical Board has its own separate Undo/Redo history, so you can experiment without affecting your main field setup.
</Callout>

## Tips for Live Tracking

<Callout type="tip">
**Keep it simple during play** - Focus on goals and subs. Add details after the game.
</Callout>

### Priority Order

1. **Goals** - Always record immediately
2. **Substitutions** - Track as they happen
3. **Fair Play Cards** - Award when you notice good behavior
4. **Notes** - Add during stoppages

### Screen Lock Prevention

MatchOps Local keeps your screen active during games. The wake lock feature:

- Prevents screen timeout
- Saves battery when paused
- Works on most devices

### Quick Access

Position your device where you can:
- See the timer without looking down
- Reach it for quick updates
- Keep it protected from weather
