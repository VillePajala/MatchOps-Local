Learn how to use MatchOps Local effectively during live matches to track events and manage your team.

## The Game Interface

When a game is active, you'll see:

- **Field View** - Your lineup positioned on the pitch
- **Timer** - Current game time and period
- **Score** - Live score display
- **Player Bar** - Bench players ready for substitution

## Recording Events

### Goals

When a goal is scored:

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Goal Log">
Click the **Goal** button in the control bar to open the Goal Log modal.
</Step>

<Step title="Select Scoring Team">
Choose whether your team or the opponent scored.
</Step>

<Step title="Record Details">
Optionally add:
- Goalscorer (select from your roster)
- Assist player
- Game time (auto-filled from timer)
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm">
Click **Save** - the score updates and the event is logged.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Substitutions

Make substitutions by dragging:

1. Drag a bench player from the Player Bar onto the field
2. Drop them on the position where you want them
3. The previous player moves to the bench automatically
4. Time played is tracked for both players

<Callout type="info">
Substitution timing is recorded automatically based on the game clock.
</Callout>

### Fair Play Cards

To award a Fair Play Card:

1. Click the **Fair Play** button in the control bar
2. Select the player being recognized
3. Add a note about the positive behavior if desired
4. Confirm

<Callout type="tip">
Fair Play Cards are **positive** awards for good sportsmanship - not disciplinary cards.
</Callout>

### Game Notes

You can add notes to capture important moments during the game using the notes feature.

## Timer Management

### Basic Controls

- **Play/Pause** - Toggle the timer using the play button (▶/⏸)
- **Period End** - Click to end the current period and advance to the next
- The timer shows elapsed time for the current period

### Period Transitions

At halftime or period end:

1. Pause the timer
2. Review period statistics
3. Make lineup changes
4. Click to advance to the next period
5. Press play to start the new period

### Stoppage Time

For stoppage time at the end of a period:

1. Keep the timer running past the period length
2. End the period manually when stoppage time is complete
3. The actual time played is recorded

## Making Adjustments

### Formation Changes

During the game, you can:

1. Drag players to new positions on the field
2. Rearrange your formation as needed
3. Changes happen instantly on screen

### Tactical Board

For more detailed tactical adjustments, use the **Tactical Board**:

1. Click the **Tactical Board** button
2. Draw plays, formations, or instructions
3. Show players during halftime discussions
4. See [Tactics Board](/guide/tactics-board) for detailed instructions

## Tips for Live Tracking

<Callout type="tip">
**Keep it simple during play** - Focus on goals and subs. Add details after the game.
</Callout>

### Priority Order

1. **Goals** - Always record immediately
2. **Substitutions** - Track as they happen
3. **Fair Play Cards** - Award when you notice good behavior
4. **Notes** - Add during stoppages

### Screen Lock Prevention

MatchOps Local keeps your screen active during games. The wake lock feature:

- Prevents screen timeout
- Saves battery when paused
- Works on most devices

### Quick Access

Position your device where you can:
- See the timer without looking down
- Reach it for quick updates
- Keep it protected from weather
