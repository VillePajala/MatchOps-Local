Welcome to MatchOps Local! This guide will help you get up and running quickly.

<Callout type="tip">
MatchOps Local works completely offline after the first load. No account needed!
</Callout>

## Installation

MatchOps Local is a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means you can install it on your device just like a regular app.

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the App">
Visit [matchops.app](https://matchops.app) in your browser (Chrome, Safari, or Edge recommended).
</Step>

<Step title="Install the PWA">
Look for the install prompt or use your browser's menu to "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App".
</Step>

<Step title="Launch">
Open the installed app from your home screen or app drawer. It works offline!
</Step>
</Steps>

## First Steps

Once installed, here's what to do first:

### 1. Set Up Your Roster

Before your first match, add your players to the roster:

1. Click **Manage Roster** in the control bar at the bottom
2. Click **Add Player** in the roster panel
3. Enter player name and jersey number in the Player Form modal
4. Repeat for all players

<Callout type="info">
You can import players from a CSV/Excel file if you have many players to add. Use the Import/Export options in the roster panel.
</Callout>

### 2. Create Your First Game

1. Click **New Game** in the control bar
2. Enter opponent name and select your team
3. Set the number of periods and period length
4. Choose game type (Soccer or Futsal)
5. Click **Create Game** to start

### 3. Select Starting Players

1. Players appear in the Player Bar below the field
2. Drag players from the bar onto the field
3. Arrange them according to your desired formation
