After the final whistle, MatchOps Local provides powerful tools to review and analyze your match data.

## Game Summary

### Immediate Review

When you end a game, you'll see:

- **Final Score** - The match result
- **Event Timeline** - Chronological list of all recorded events
- **Quick Stats** - Goals, Fair Play Cards, and substitutions

### Accessing Past Games

To review earlier games:

1. Go to **Games** or **History**
2. Select the game you want to review
3. All data remains accessible

## Statistics

### Team Statistics

View overall team performance:

- Goals scored/conceded
- Total substitutions
- Fair Play Cards awarded

### Player Statistics

For each player, see:

- **Time Played** - Total minutes on the field
- **Goals** - Goals scored
- **Assists** - Goal assists
- **Fair Play Cards** - Positive sportsmanship awards received

<Callout type="info">
Player statistics accumulate across seasons when games are linked to seasons.
</Callout>

## Analysis Tools

### Event Timeline

The timeline shows every recorded event:

- Timestamp
- Event type
- Players involved
- Notes

Filter by:
- Event type (goals, subs, Fair Play Cards)
- Player
- Time period

### Playing Time Analysis

Understanding playing time:

- Total minutes per player
- Percentage of game played
- When they were on/off the field
- Substitution patterns

### Performance Insights

Look for patterns:

- Who plays most consistently?
- Which lineup combinations work best?
- When do you concede goals?
- Substitution timing effectiveness

## Exporting Data

### Excel Export

Export game data to Excel:

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the Game">
Navigate to the completed game.
</Step>

<Step title="Select Export">
Tap the export button or menu option.
</Step>

<Step title="Choose Format">
Select Excel (.xlsx) format.
</Step>

<Step title="Save">
Choose where to save the file.
</Step>
</Steps>

### What's Included

The Excel export contains:

- Game details and score
- Complete event timeline
- Player statistics
- Playing time breakdown

### Season Reports

Export entire seasons:

- Cumulative statistics
- Player rankings
- Game-by-game results
- Trend analysis data

## Post-Game Routine

### Recommended Steps

<Steps>
<Step title="Verify Events">
Check that all goals and major events are recorded correctly.
</Step>

<Step title="Add Notes">
Add any additional context while it's fresh.
</Step>

<Step title="Review Stats">
Look at playing time and key statistics.
</Step>

<Step title="Export if Needed">
Save data externally for backup or sharing.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Sharing with Others

You can share:

- Screenshot of final score
- Exported Excel files
- Event timeline summary

<Callout type="tip">
Create a post-game routine to ensure you capture all important information before forgetting details.
</Callout>

## Long-Term Tracking

Over time, your data enables:

- Season performance comparison
- Player development tracking
- Team improvement metrics
- Historical reference
