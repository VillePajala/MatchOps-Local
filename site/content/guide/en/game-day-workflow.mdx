This guide walks you through the complete workflow for a match day, from preparation to post-game review.

## Before the Game

### 1. Create or Resume a Game

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the App">
Launch MatchOps Local. If you have a game in progress, you'll see the option to resume it.
</Step>

<Step title="Create New Game">
Tap **New Game** to set up a new match. You'll need to enter:
- Your team name
- Opponent name
- Number of periods
- Period length
- Game type (Soccer/Futsal)
</Step>

<Step title="Associate with Season">
Optionally link the game to a season or tournament to track standings and statistics over time.
</Step>
</Steps>

### 2. Set Your Lineup

Once your game is created:

1. **Access your roster** from the Player Bar at the bottom
2. **Drag players** onto the field to set your starting lineup
3. **Position players** according to your formation

<Callout type="tip">
Set up your lineup before arriving at the field to save time during warmups.
</Callout>

## During the Game

### Starting the Match

1. Tap the **Play** button (â–¶) in the timer area to start the clock
2. The period clock begins counting
3. You're now ready to track events

### Tracking Events

During play, you can record:

- **Goals** - Click the **Goal** button in the control bar to open the Goal Log modal
- **Substitutions** - Swap players on and off the field by dragging
- **Fair Play Cards** - Award positive sportsmanship recognition to players
- **Notes** - Add text notes for specific moments

<Callout type="info">
Fair Play Cards are **positive** awards for good sportsmanship, not disciplinary cards like yellow/red cards in official matches.
</Callout>

### Using the Timer

- **Play/Pause** - Toggle the timer with the play button
- **Period End** - Click to advance to the next period
- The timer shows elapsed time for the current period

### Making Substitutions

<Steps>
<Step title="Drag from Bench">
Drag a player from the Player Bar onto the field position where you want them.
</Step>

<Step title="Replace Field Player">
The player currently in that position moves to the bench automatically.
</Step>

<Step title="Automatic Recording">
The substitution is recorded with the current game time.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Halftime

When the period ends:

1. Pause the timer
2. Review first-half statistics
3. Make tactical adjustments
4. Prepare your second-half lineup
5. Use the **Tactical Board** for team talk visualizations

<Callout type="info">
Your lineup and all events are automatically saved. You can safely close the app during halftime.
</Callout>

## After the Game

### Ending the Match

1. Tap **End Game** when the final whistle blows
2. Confirm the final score
3. The game is saved to your history

### Review and Analysis

After ending the game:

- View the complete event timeline
- Check player statistics
- Review time played by each player
- Export data to Excel if needed

See the [After the Game](/guide/after-the-game) guide for detailed analysis options.
