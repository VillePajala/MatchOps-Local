## Full Post (Reddit / Facebook Groups / Forums)

**Looking for beta testers: MatchOps — a soccer & futsal coaching app for game day**

Hi everyone! I'm a solo developer from Finland and I've been building **MatchOps**, a mobile app specifically designed for youth soccer and futsal coaches to handle everything that happens on game day — from lineups and formations to live game tracking and post-game assessments.

I've been coaching youth teams myself and got tired of juggling paper lineups, mental notes about who's played how long, and trying to remember everything after the game. So I built the tool I wished I had on the sideline.

### What the app does

**Pre-game preparation:**
- Create and manage your player roster with jersey numbers and positions
- Set up formations on an interactive soccer/futsal field — tap a player, tap the field to place them
- Plan your starting lineup and substitutions before the whistle
- Organize your season with teams, seasons, and tournaments

**Live game tracking:**
- Built-in game timer with period management (configurable period length)
- Live score tracking
- Substitution management — see at a glance who's on the field and who's on the bench
- Player time tracking — know exactly how many minutes each player has played
- Game event logging (goals, cards, substitutions) with timestamps
- Tactical board with drawings and disc placement for quick adjustments

**Post-game review:**
- Player assessment system with customizable sliders (intensity, technique, teamwork, creativity, and more)
- Full game event history with timeline
- Save and review past games anytime
- Export game data to Excel for further analysis

**Works offline, your data stays yours:**
- The app works completely offline — all data is stored on your device
- Optional cloud sync if you want to access your data across multiple devices
- No mandatory account required for local mode

**Supports both soccer and futsal:**
- Soccer (11v11) and futsal field layouts
- Game settings adapt to each format (period length, field size, rules)

**Available in English and Finnish** (more languages planned based on demand).

### What I'm looking for

I'm running a **closed beta test on Google Play** and I need **20 testers** to help me find bugs, test on different devices, and give me feedback on the user experience before public launch.

**You'll get:**
- Free early access to the full app
- Direct line to the developer — your feedback shapes the product
- A say in what features get built next

**What I ask in return:**
- Use the app during at least one real game or practice session
- Tell me what works, what doesn't, and what's confusing
- Report any crashes or bugs you encounter
- It takes about 5 minutes to set up your first roster and game

### Who is this app for?

- Youth soccer or futsal coaches at any level (recreational to competitive)
- Parents who volunteer-coach their kids' teams
- Assistant coaches who handle lineups and subs
- Anyone who wants a better way to manage game day than pen and paper

### How to join the beta

1. **DM me your Gmail address** (the one linked to your Google Play account)
2. I'll add you to the closed testing group — you'll get a Play Store link within 24h
3. Install from Google Play and start using it at your next game!

**Important:** I need to manually add each tester's email to Google Play Console, so please send me the **Gmail address you use on your Android phone**. The testing link won't work without this step.

**Requirements:** Android phone (any modern version).

Thanks for reading — I'd really appreciate your help making this the best sideline tool for coaches!
